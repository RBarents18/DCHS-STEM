Principles of AI

Exploring Artificial Intelligence through a Faith-Based Lens

"The heart of the discerning acquires knowledge, for the ears of the wise seek it out." — Proverbs 18:15

About This Course

What Is Principles of AI?

Principles of AI introduces students to the foundational concepts behind modern artificial intelligence — from how machines learn to make predictions, to the ethical questions raised by AI in society. Rooted in a Christ-centered worldview, this course equips students to think critically and responsibly about technology.

🧠

Machine Learning

Discover how computers learn from data using models like the perceptron, the building block of modern neural networks.

📊

Data & Models

Understand how data is used to train, test, and validate AI systems — and why data quality matters.

⚖️

AI Ethics

Explore the moral dimensions of AI — bias, fairness, privacy, and the responsibility that comes with powerful tools.

✝️

Faith & Technology

Reflect on what it means to be made in the image of a creative God as we build systems that can reason, predict, and generate.

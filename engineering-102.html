Engineering 102

University of Arizona Dual Enrollment Program

"Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord, not for human masters." - Colossians 3:23

Program Overview

Engineering 102 (ENGR 102) is an exciting dual enrollment opportunity that allows Desert Christian high school students to earn University of Arizona college credits while still in high school. This program provides a hands-on introduction to engineering principles and practices.

Course Details

Course Credit

Earn 3 University of Arizona credits (ENGR 102) that can be applied toward your future college degree.

Curriculum Focus

Introduction to engineering design process, technical communication, teamwork, and hands-on project development.

Prerequisites

Open to motivated high school students with an interest in engineering. Strong math and science background recommended.

Program Duration

Full academic year program with semester-based projects and assessments aligned with university standards.
